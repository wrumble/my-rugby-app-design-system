Forms use vanilla Material 3 OutlineInputBorder with our pill FilledButton/TextButton overrides. Validators are i18n'd (auth.validate_*). Submit-state buttons swap label for an inline CircularProgressIndicator with the matching auth.signing_in_a11y Semantics label. SwitchListTile rows live inside an AppCard with padding: EdgeInsets.zero and a Divider(indent:16, endIndent:16) between rows. Section headings use theme.textTheme.titleSmall uppercase with letterSpacing: 0.5, NOT Bebas Neue — Bebas is reserved for in-content section titles via AppSection.
