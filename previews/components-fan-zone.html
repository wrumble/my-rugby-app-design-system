Fan Zone widgets all wrap in AppCard on the Match Hub navy surface (#1A1A2E). Section titles use the local SectionHeader primitive (Bebas Neue display-sm, 24pt). Vote/predict buttons fire MatchHubAnalyticsService.logPotmVoted() / logPredictionMade() respectively — never bypass it. Progress bars use AppColors.red500 (#E63946, brightened to #FF6B78 in high-contrast mode) — semantic accent for Match Hub. Reactions use 5 emoji (🔥💪😱😢😡) — note: this is the only place emoji are used in the app outside of match-event icons. OtherScoresPanel live dot is the same red pulsing 900ms cycle as the LIVE badge.
